Pan-Seared Steak

Ingredients

  • Your choice of steak.
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 sprigs of rosemary
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 1/2 lemon

Instructions

  1. Pat the steaks dry 30 minutes prior to cooking.
  2. Season liberally with salt and black pepper.
  3. Preheat a saute pan over medium heat, adding in 1 tbsp butter.
  4. Place the steak in the preheated pan, and add in the remaining tbsp of butter on top of the steak.
  5. Add the garlic cloves and rosemary into the pan.
  6. Tilt the pan to let the aromatics season the butter, and spoon the butter over the steaek as it sears.
  7. Remove the steak from the pan, allow it to rest for 5 minutes, and serve with a spoon of aromatic butter.
