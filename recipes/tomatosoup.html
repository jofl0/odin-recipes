Creamy Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 3-6 fresh campari and plum tomatoes
  • 1 whole white onion
  • 8 cloves garlic, crushed
  • fresh basil to taste
  • 16 oz. chicken broth
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • heavy/whipping cream

Instructions

  1. Roast tomatoes, garlic, and onion - toss ingredients in olive oil with salt and pepper and roast for 50 minutes in the oven.
  2. Add all ingredients into a large pot, combine with chicken broth, and cook down to desired consistency.
  3. Use an immersion blender to blend the tomatoes, garlic, and onion into a creamy texture.
  4. Add in heavy whipping cream and salt/pepper to taste.
  5. Ladle the soup into a bowl, and serve with fresh bread and cheese.
